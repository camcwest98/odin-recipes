Recipe

Time to Complete: Depends on Freezer

  • Water
  • Ice Cube Trays
  1. Divide water amongst ice cube trays
  2. Place in Freezer
  3. Chill water until at least -32 °F or 0 °C
  4. Remove cubes from trays
  5. Enjoy!
